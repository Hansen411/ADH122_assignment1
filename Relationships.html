Relationship Chart!

Pretty Little Liars —like many teen dramas— is no stranger to large and interconnected webs of relationships.
Below you will find an interconnected web of relationships that occur duing the 7 seasons of of the show.

Clearly, Pretty Little Liars has no shortage of romantic drama. From secret relationships to love triangles to cheating scandals, the show has it all. Whether you're a fan of the main characters or just love a good romance, there's something for everyone in the tangled web of relationships that make up the world of Pretty Little Liars.

Important Notes:

  • What Type of Relationships Are Depicted?

    This chart does not include familial relationships, only romantic ones.

  • What Qualifies a Relationship For This Chart?

    This chart includes relationships that were shown on screen as well as those that were mentioned, only if the relationship mentioned is meaningful to the story.

  • What Constitutes a Meaningful Mentioned Relationship?

    A meaninful relationship could include having a child with a character that has never been shown on screen or a character cheating on their partner off screen.

  • What Relationships Are NOT Included?

    This chart does not include relationships that were only hinted at or implied.

  • Why Are Some Dots Dark Blue?

    This indicates that a side character has had a relationship with two or more of the main characters.
